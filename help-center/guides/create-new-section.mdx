---
title: Create a New Section
description: How to add a new section in yashwanth-test2.
---

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Project Dashboard">
    Go to your yashwanth-test2 dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'Add Section'">
    Find and click the 'Add Section' button in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name Your Section">
    Enter a section name (e.g., Guides) and save.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Pages">
    Add new documentation pages into your section.
  </Step>
</Steps>
