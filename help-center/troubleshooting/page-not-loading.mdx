---
title: Page Not Loading
description: Fix when a documentation page won’t load.
---

## Symptoms

- Blank screen when opening a doc page
- Error: "Page Not Found" or endless loading

## Steps to Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Refresh browser">
    Press Ctrl+R or Cmd+R to reload.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check URL">
    Make sure you’re visiting a valid page link.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear cache">
    Clear browser cache and cookies, then retry.
  </Step>
</Steps>
